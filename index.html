About Me

My name is Molly Zurheide. I’m a front-end web developer based in Portsmouth, New Hampshire with a wide-ranging educational and occupational background. To me, technology is exhilarating, and I love being part of a field that is revolutionizing the way we live. As an adolescent, I taught myself HTML and CSS because I loved using my artistic sensibilities to create content. It is my enthusiasm and insatiable appetite for learning that drive me to succeed in this field today. Though I don’t come from a coding background, I possess skills and traits that make me well suited for development. I am a top-notch problem solver, persistent, meticulously organized, creative, and easy to work with. My portfolio demonstrates my front-end expertise and my eye for attractive and intuitive design. My primary interests as a developer include optimizing interface to improve the user experience and front-end work with Javascript and jQuery.
